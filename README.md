# CVision

# Company Interface - Screenshots

This branch contains screenshots of the company dashboard and all management pages.

## [Dashboard](dashboard.jpeg)
Shows statistics, charts, active job offers, and recent applications. A "Request Ad" button is located at the top.

## [Request Ad](request_ad.jpeg)
Popup window to upload an image and provide a link URL. The request is sent to the admin for approval or rejection.

## [Company Profile](company_profile.jpeg)
Displays all company information entered during registration.

## [Edit Company Profile](company_profile_edit.jpeg)
Allows editing of company information. (Admin account info and uploaded documents cannot be modified here.)

## [Create Job](create_job.jpeg)
Form to create a new job offer with title, description, requirements, responsibilities, and an option to enable a linked test. Includes a button to publish the job.

## [Job Offers](job_offer.jpeg)
Lists all job offers created by the company with their status (active or closed).

## [Applicants](applicants.jpeg)
Displays all candidates who applied, showing compatibility score, test status, test result, and application status (accepted, rejected). Includes a button to view the candidate's CV.

## [Candidate CV](cv.jpeg)
Shows full candidate information, compatibility score, and current status (accepted, rejected, pending). For pending applications, Approve and Reject buttons appear.

## [Company Accept](company_accept.jpeg)
Popup window to confirm acceptance of a candidate.

## [Company Reject](company_reject.jpeg)
Popup window to confirm rejection of a candidate.

## [Recruitment](recruitment.jpeg)
Shows statistics, hiring progress by position, and candidates grouped by status (pending, rejected, accepted). Includes a button to start a conversation with a candidate.

## [Tests](test.jpeg)
Displays statistics and lists all tests created by the company.

## [Create Test](create_test.jpeg)
Form to create an MCQ test linked to a specific job offer. Includes question text, four answer options, correct answer selection, duration, and passing score.

## [Create Training](create_training.jprg)
Form to create a training program linked to a specific job offer. Contains multiple modules, each can include images, videos, text, files, or MCQ quizzes. Includes a publish button.

## [Training Published](training_publish.jprg)
The page showing the training after it has been published.

## [Trainings List](training.jpeg)
Lists all training programs created by the company with their details.

## [Messages](messages.jpeg)
Accessed from the recruitment page conversation icon. Allows communication with a specific candidate. Supports files, emojis, and messages. (Note: The company initiates the conversation.)

## Settings
- [**General Settings:**](setting_general.jpeg) Change company admin name and email.
- [**Notification Settings:**](setting_notification.jpeg) Enable or disable email notifications for new applications and test completions.
- [**Security Settings:**](setting_security.jpeg) Change password.
- [**Account Settings:**](setting_account.jpeg) Delete company account button.

## [Delete Account](delete_company.jpeg)
Popup window asking the user to type "DELETE" to confirm account deletion.
